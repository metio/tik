# Runbook: hypothesis / :stated

Assert `statement` (the falsifiable claim) and `kill` (what counts as
losing, decided NOW, before any work). Write the kill criterion as an
observable event, not a feeling: 'abandoned within a month', 'requires
prompt-side enforcement'. The process will not let a verdict through
without this — that is the point.
